East Bay Model United Nations Conference

About

East Bay Model United Nations is an annually held event run that invites hundreds of delegates from all over the bay area and beyond to participate in a weekend of debate, education, and growth. The conference, held at the award-winning California High School, is run by high school students ensuring a personal and unique spin on the MUN format. This website will be updated regularly with further details.
